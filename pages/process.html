S.P.A.C.E Organizing
What we do in a nutshell:

S.P.A.C.E. Organization helps you intentionally organize your surroundings and create individualized systems to help get you on track and teach you how to maintain on your own. We achieve this using The 5 C's of Organization™ (a method developed by Shannyn):

  • Consider your needs (most important step- sets the stage for the final outcome)
  • Categorize your belongings
  • Cleanse what doesn't serve you
  • Contain what does serve you
  • Customize to suit your individual needs and limitations

What you can expect the steps of the process to look like

  1. Schedule a consultation- Requires approval. Please allow up to 48 hrs to get confirmation of your appt.
  2. Fill out the Intake Form- This will be in the inbox of the email you provided, with your appt confirmation.
  3. Consultation- We tour the space(s) and talk about your needs and what is not working for you. Should you choose to move forward we will sign the Scope of Project Contract at this stage.
  4. Develop the plan- Shannyn will take home photos and notes from our discussion to develop your customized plan.
  5. Sign Scope of Project Contract- Once you approve the plan you sign the contract that includes the scope, price and timeline of the project and I collect the deposit.
  6. Do the work!- I come into your space and implement the approved plan.
  7. Orient the client- You come into the space and I orient you to where your things are and the systems I have implemented
  8. Collect final payment
  9. Provide a testimonial and refer your friends!
